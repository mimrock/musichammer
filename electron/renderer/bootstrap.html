MUSIC HAMMER
First-run setup

MusicHammer runs its audio engine locally, and that engine has to be downloaded once: Python, PyTorch and the separation stack — about to download, of disk space once installed.

An NVIDIA GPU was detected, so the larger CUDA build will be installed — separations will run many times faster.

Avoid doing this on a metered or mobile connection. Nothing has been downloaded yet — you can safely quit and come back on a better network.

Separation models (≈0.7 GB each) are not part of this — you pick and download those later, inside the app.
